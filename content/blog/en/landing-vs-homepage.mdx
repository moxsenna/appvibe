Many businesses direct every ad campaign (Meta Ads, Google Ads, or promotions) straight to their homepage. Traffic increases, but WhatsApp conversations and actual conversions often stay flat.

The homepage is important, but it is rarely the best place to turn paid visitors into leads.

## Why the homepage usually fails for paid traffic

A homepage is built to serve **everyone**. It needs to introduce multiple services, showcase portfolio, display testimonials, and provide full navigation. This creates several problems:

- The ad message does not match the page content (message mismatch)
- Visitors are unsure which action to take
- Too many competing CTAs
- Slower load time because of heavy content

A dedicated landing page, on the other hand, has **one single goal**: get the visitor to perform one primary action (usually starting a WhatsApp chat).

## When you should create a dedicated landing page

Build a separate landing page when:

- You are running paid advertising (Meta, Google, TikTok Ads)
- The campaign promotes a specific offer (discount, event, limited package)
- You have different audience segments (e.g., acne consultation vs general skin check)
- You want to test messaging or design quickly

## Key differences

| Aspect             | Homepage                              | Dedicated Landing Page                     |
|--------------------|---------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| Goal               | Introduce the brand broadly           | Convert one traffic source into leads      |
| CTA                | Multiple options                      | One primary button (usually WhatsApp)      |
| Messaging          | General                               | Matches the exact ad that was clicked      |
| Load speed         | Slower                                | Faster                                     |
| Tracking           | Hard to measure per campaign          | Easy to measure (one page = one campaign)  |

## Quick checklist: do you need a new landing page?

- [ ] Ads are running but conversion is low
- [ ] Different traffic sources are sent to the same page
- [ ] Homepage has more than two primary CTAs
- [ ] Homepage takes more than 3 seconds to load on mobile
- [ ] Difficult to measure ROI per campaign

If three or more boxes are checked, you likely need a dedicated landing page.

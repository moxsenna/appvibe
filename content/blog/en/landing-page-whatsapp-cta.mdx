## The usual gap

Many teams already have a landing page, but the primary **“Contact us”** action is vague: a form that rarely gets filled, or a bare WhatsApp number with no opener. Visitors hesitate — and nothing gets sent.

## What we implement

1. **One primary action** above the fold: a WhatsApp button with a specific label, e.g. *“Consult on WhatsApp”*, not *“Click here”*.
2. **Prefilled WhatsApp text** — business name and a short intent included; visitors only tap send.
3. **Proof near the top** — service scope, region, or a response-time promise (e.g. weekday reply within 24 hours).
4. **Ad-specific landings** — one campaign → one page → one CTA, not a generic homepage.

## Five-minute checklist

- [ ] WhatsApp button visible on mobile without excessive scrolling  
- [ ] Meta title/description state **who** the offer is for  
- [ ] Hero does not compete with three CTAs (WA, phone, email)

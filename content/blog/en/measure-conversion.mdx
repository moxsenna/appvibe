Many business owners feel their landing page is “good enough,” but they have no idea whether it actually produces results. They don’t have expensive analytics tools, so they end up guessing.

The truth is, you don’t need complex tools to measure whether a landing page works. You only need a few simple numbers that can be tracked manually.

## Why You Need to Measure

Without measurement, you won’t know:
- Whether your ads are actually profitable
- Which part of the landing page needs improvement
- How much it costs on average to get one lead

## Simple Measurement Framework (4 Key Metrics)

### 1. Traffic → Chat (WhatsApp Button Click Rate)

**How to calculate:**
```
Number of WhatsApp button clicks ÷ Total page visitors × 100
```

**Good target:** 8–15% for service businesses.

**How to track:** Use UTM parameters in ads + manual recording in Google Sheets, or use Meta Ads “Link Click” data.

### 2. Chat → Qualified Lead (Response Rate)

**How to calculate:**
```
Number of chats replied to within 30 minutes ÷ Total incoming chats × 100
```

**Good target:** At least 70%.

This metric is often ignored, yet it has a huge impact on final conversion.

### 3. Lead → Deal (Final Conversion Rate)

**How to calculate:**
```
Number of deals closed ÷ Total leads received × 100
```

**Good target:** 15–30%, depending on the type of business.

### 4. Cost Per Lead

**How to calculate:**
```
Total ad spend ÷ Number of leads generated
```

Example: Rp 1,500,000 in ads generates 45 leads → Cost per lead = Rp 33,333.

## Free Tools You Can Use

| Need                    | Free Tool                        | Notes |
|-------------------------|----------------------------------|-------|
| Track button clicks     | Meta Pixel / Google Tag Manager  | Can be set up yourself |
| Record chats & deals    | Google Sheets                    | Manual but sufficient |
| UTM tracking            | Google Analytics (free)          | Track traffic sources |
| Response time           | Manual spreadsheet recording     | Simplest method |

## Weekly Measurement Checklist

- [ ] Record number of WhatsApp button clicks
- [ ] Count how many chats came in
- [ ] Track how many chats were replied to within 30 minutes
- [ ] Record how many leads turned into closed deals
- [ ] Calculate cost per lead for the week

Do this every week for 4–6 weeks. You will start seeing patterns and know what needs to be fixed.
